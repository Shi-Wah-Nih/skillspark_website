FAQ

What is Skill Spark?

Skill Spark is an IT training institute in Nepal offering affordable, industry-relevant courses with certifications to help you excel in the IT industry.

Where is Skill Spark located?

Skill Spark is located at Shankhamul Tower, 6th Floor, Kathmandu, Nepal. Visit our website or contact us for more location details.

What courses does Skill Spark offer?

We provide a wide range of IT courses, including web development, software engineering, data science, cybersecurity, and more.

What are the qualifications of Skill Spark instructors?

Our instructors are industry professionals with extensive experience, ensuring practical and up-to-date training.

What are the fees for Skill Spark courses?

Course fees vary based on the course and mode of learning; detailed pricing is available on the course pages.

How do I enroll in a course at Skill Spark?

To enroll, visit the Course page, choose your preferred course and mode of learning (in-person or online), and follow the registration process.

How long do the courses typically last?

Courses usually range from a few weeks to several months, depending on the subject and intensity.

Do I need prior IT knowledge to enroll in your courses?

No prior IT knowledge is needed for many beginner-level courses; advanced courses may have prerequisites.

Do you offer any financial assistance or scholarships?

Yes, we offer financial assistance and scholarships to eligible students; check our website for application details.

Does Skill Spark offer job placement assistance?

Yes, we provide job placement support, including job listings, resume building, and interview preparation.
